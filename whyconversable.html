Why Conversable

ConversABLE is a transformational movement that operates under a social enterprise model focused on offering empathic communication services. We validate, everyday, that empathy has the power to transform human relationships, and therefore the world.

Our vision is to facilitate the tools and the practice of Nonviolent Communication to as many people as possible, thus generating a critical mass of change agents. We promote a world where emotional authenticity is the greatest source of collaboration, tolerance and human connection.

We work with empathy as a vehicle to create sustainable solutions for innovative organizations and conscientious individuals.

Our Team

ConversABLE is led by a passionate group of changemakers.

Christine Raine

Founding Director

christine@conversable.org

Since she was a child, Christine's need for adventure and truth has taken her around the globe. The legacy: empathy has the power to change the world. Her mission: to prove it. Christine received an honors degree from New York University while studying International Relations and Psychology, and worked on a wide variety of social projects and organizations thereafter as a researcher, activist, program manager and trainer. After receiving her Master's degree in Medical Anthropology in London, and five years in the Costa Rican folk band Passiflora, she took the leap of faith that gave life to ConversABLE. Her clients are as diverse as the Costa Rican government, private companies like Ernst & Young, vulnerable communities and the United Nations. Christine is also the co-founder of WONDERMORE: Inner journeys, outside. Her vision is to actively co-create a world where people can share their truth with love and courage, and connect with each other from the heart.

Camilo Chaves

Director of Operations

camilo@conversable.org

Camilo is a psychologist with broad project management experience overseeing intercultural education programs in Latin America. He feels passionate about the application and development of new technologies. His experiences living in Spain, Norway and Costa Rica have contributed to his desire to learn about human diversity, in all of its historical, cultural and visual facets. He also enjoys artistic expression through drawing, painting and photography. Camilo is in charge of everything related to operations and making dreams happen in ConversABLE, a world-class juggling act indeed.

Shanti Vidya

Director of Strategic Education

shanti@conversable.org

Shanti holds a PHD in Peace and Communication, and has worked in the field of education for over 20 years. Her experiences have taken her from Cape Verde, Africa, where she served as a bridge between the government and rural communities, to Long Island University as a professor and Faculty Advisor for the Global Studies Program in Costa Rica. Shanti also worked as a trainer and evaluator in the initial stages of a pioneering peace education project in public schools, led by the Academy for Peace. This experience fired up her passion for empathic communication as a powerful vehicle for social transformation. She feels ecstatic to be able to contribute with these tools once again from ConversABLE.

Jose (Chino) Salom

Director of Educational Management

chino@conversable.org

Jose, aka “Chino”, is the founder of Salom Arquitectura, and professor of architecture at Veritas University. He received his Master's degree from the University of Buenos Aires, where his passion for teaching began as professor of the Cátedra de Solsona at the UBA, in Buenos Aires. He collaborated with the architecture firm a77, in the design and construction of urban equipment projects for low income and marginal communities, in the outskirts of the Argentine capital. In Costa Rica, he has supported and designed various social projects alongside organizations like Habitat for Humanity. Since 2014 he is an active member and facilitator of ConversABLE. His vision is to universalize emotional intelligence, regardless of social classes.
